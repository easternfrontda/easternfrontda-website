## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Wait until after LLC formation** - Use final business name
2. **Wait until business email is active** - Don't print old email
3. **Order matte finish** - Easier to write notes on back
4. **Include QR code (optional)** - Links directly to your website
5. **Keep it simple** - Don't overcrowd the card
6. **Proofread 3 times** - Errors on 250 cards are expensive
7. **Order samples first** - Many printers offer 10-25 sample cards
