### Step 1: Click Publish Tab
Located at the top of this interface

### Step 2: Choose Platform
- **Netlify** (Recommended - Free, automatic HTTPS, forms)
- **Vercel** (Free, fast, easy custom domains)
- **Traditional Hosting** (Your own hosting provider)

### Step 3: Deploy
- Netlify/Vercel: Follow prompts, site goes live in ~2 minutes
- Traditional: Download files, upload via FTP

### Step 4: Custom Domain
- Point easternfrontda.com to your hosting

- Enable HTTPS/SSL certificate
