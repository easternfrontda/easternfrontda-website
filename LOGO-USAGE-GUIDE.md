## 📐 Logo Specifications

### **Minimum Sizes:**
- **Print:** 2 inches wide minimum
- **Digital:** 200px wide minimum
- **Social media icons:** 500x500px (crop to square)

### **Safe Space:**
- Keep at least 0.25" (20px) clear space around logo
- Don't crowd with text or other graphics

### **File Formats:**
- **PNG** - For web, presentations, most digital uses (transparent background supported)
- **JPG** - For photos, email (smaller file size, no transparency)
- **SVG** - For print, large format (infinite scaling, best quality)

## ✅ DO's

✅ **Use the correct logo version for each application**  
✅ **Maintain proper proportions** (don't stretch or squish)  
✅ **Ensure adequate clear space** around the logo  
✅ **Use on appropriate backgrounds** (dark logos on light, light on dark)  
✅ **Keep logos sharp and crisp** (use high-resolution files)  
✅ **Match brand colors exactly** (use hex codes provided)

---

## ❌ DON'Ts

❌ **Don't distort or stretch** the logo  
❌ **Don't change colors** (except white/black for one-color printing)  
❌ **Don't add effects** (shadows, glows, bevels, gradients)  
❌ **Don't rotate** the logo at angles  
❌ **Don't place on busy backgrounds** (logo must be clearly visible)  
❌ **Don't recreate or modify** the logo design  
❌ **Don't use low-resolution** or pixelated versions  
❌ **Don't place too small** (below minimum sizes)

## ✨ Special Use Cases

### **One-Color Printing (Black & White):**
- Use solid black version
- No gradients or colors
- Maintain all design elements

### **Embroidery:**
- Simplify to 2-3 colors max
- Increase spacing between elements
- Consider removing small details

### **Engraving:**
- Use outline version only
- Remove all fills and colors
- Increase line weights
