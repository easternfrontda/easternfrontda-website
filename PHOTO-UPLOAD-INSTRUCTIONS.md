## 🚨 IMPORTANT: Face Blurring

**Before uploading any photos with other people visible, you MUST blur their faces.**

### Why?
- Privacy protection
- Legal liability (model releases)
- Professional ethics

### How to blur faces:

#### **Option 1: Canva (Easiest)**
1. Go to Canva.com
2. Upload your photo
3. Click "Edit Image" → "Effects" → "Blur"
4. Manually blur each face that's NOT yours
5. Download and use

#### **Option 2: Photopea (Free Photoshop Alternative)**
1. Go to Photopea.com
2. Upload photo
3. Select the Blur Tool
4. Paint over each face
5. Export as JPG/PNG

#### **Option 3: Online Blur Tool**
- RedKetchup.io/blur-image
- Pinetools.com/blur-image
- LunaPic.com

## 📋 Photo Selection Checklist

When choosing photos from your collection, pick ones that are:

✅ **High Quality**
- Clear, well-lit
- Not blurry or pixelated
- Good composition

✅ **Professional**
- Proper safety protocols visible
- Organized training environment
- Professional attire/gear

✅ **Action-Oriented**
- Shows real training in progress
- Dynamic movement or instruction
- Engaging and interesting

✅ **Privacy-Protected**
- Your face can be clearly visible
- All other faces must be blurred
- No identifiable minors without guardian consent

❌ **Avoid**
- Blurry or dark photos
- Messy or unprofessional backgrounds
- Photos where safety protocols aren't visible
- Unblurred faces of students/clients
