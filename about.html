FOUNDER & CHIEF INSTRUCTOR

Matthew Stanley

Army National Guard Veteran • Ukraine Combat Veteran • NRA & USCCA Certified

Matthew Stanley brings 13 years of real-world military and security experience to Eastern Front Defense Academy. His expertise isn't just taught from manuals—it's forged through actual combat operations and training thousands of personnel across multiple continents.

Combat Experience: Matthew served with the U.S. Army National Guard infantry and deployed as a military contractor for NATO operations. From 2022-2025, he was embedded with Ukrainian forces during active combat operations, including the Kharkiv counter-offensive, waterborne assault operations near Enerhodar, and long-range reconnaissance missions in the Zaporizhia region.

Training Background: Over the course of his career, Matthew has trained over 10,000 soldiers, law enforcement officers, and civilian personnel. His instruction methods are informed by direct combat experience, making his courses uniquely practical and relevant for real-world defensive scenarios.

Professional Credentials: Matthew holds NRA certification (completing in June 2025) and USCCA certification. He has worked as a maritime security contractor, NATO military contractor, and intelligence analyst across conflict zones worldwide.

Why Train With Matthew: Most firearms instructors teach theory. Matthew teaches what actually works when your life depends on it. His courses blend military-grade tactics with civilian-appropriate applications, ensuring you develop skills that are both effective and legally sound.
