FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need To Know About Training With Us

General Questions

What makes Eastern Front Defense Academy different from other firearms training?
Our instruction is based on real combat experience, not just range theory. Matthew Stanley has 13 years of operational experience including combat operations in Ukraine (2022-2025), where he trained 10,000+ personnel. Our techniques are combat-proven and adapted for civilian defensive use.
Do I need prior firearms experience?
No! We offer courses for all skill levels from complete beginners to advanced shooters. Our Pistol Safety & Fundamentals and Rifle Safety & Fundamentals courses are perfect for first-time shooters.
Where are classes held?
We operate as a mobile training service in the Fort Wayne, Indiana area. The specific range location will be confirmed when you book your course. We use professional ranges that meet our safety and training standards.
What is your class size?
We maintain a maximum of 8 students per class to ensure personalized instruction and safety. Small class sizes allow for individual attention and hands-on coaching.

Booking & Pricing

How much do courses cost?
Course prices range from $100-$250 depending on duration and complexity. Beginner courses (4-5 hours) start at $125. Advanced courses (8 hours) are $225-$250. Check the Courses page for specific pricing.
How do I book a course?
You can book directly through our booking page, call (260) 209-4178, or email info@easternfrontda.com. We'll confirm your date, location, and send you a pre-course checklist.
Do you offer private or group training?
Yes! We offer private one-on-one instruction and custom group training for families, friends, or organizations. Contact us at (260) 209-4178 for a custom quote.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be rescheduled once at no charge. No-shows forfeit payment.

What To Bring

What equipment do I need for a pistol course?
Required:
• Firearm (semi-auto pistol or revolver)
• 200-300 rounds of ammunition
• Eye protection (shooting glasses)
• Ear protection (electronic preferred)
• Holster (OWB or IWB)

Recommended:
• At least 2 magazines (semi-auto)
• Magazine pouch
• Hat with brim
• Weather-appropriate clothing
• Water bottle and snacks
What if I don't own a firearm yet?
Contact us in advance. We may be able to arrange a rental firearm for your first course. However, we strongly recommend taking a beginner course with a firearm you plan to own.
Can I rent equipment at the range?
Most ranges we use offer firearm rentals and sell ammunition. However, availability varies. We recommend bringing your own equipment when possible.

Safety & Requirements

Is firearms training safe?
Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. All courses begin with comprehensive safety briefings. We maintain strict range safety protocols and a maximum 8:1 student-to-instructor ratio. Our instructor has 13 years of professional firearms experience.
What is the minimum age for training?
Students must be at least 18 years old for pistol courses and 16 years old for rifle courses (with parental consent if under 18). Youth training options available—contact us for details.
Do I need a permit to take a course?
No permit is required to take our courses. However, you must be legally eligible to possess a firearm under federal and Indiana state law. Concealed carry permits are covered in our P4: Concealed Carry Mastery course.
What is your weather policy?
Most courses proceed rain or shine (we train in all conditions). Severe weather (lightning, extreme cold) may require rescheduling. You'll be notified by phone/email if weather affects your course.

Course-Specific Questions

Can I take courses in any order?
We recommend following the progression (P1→P2→P3, etc.), but it's not mandatory. If you have prior experience, we can recommend the appropriate starting point. Contact us to discuss your skill level.
Do you provide certification?
Yes. Upon successful completion of any course, you receive a certificate of completion from Eastern Front Defense Academy. Our NRA certification courses (available June 2025) will provide NRA credentials.
What if I need to leave a course early?
If you must leave early due to an emergency, please inform the instructor. However, partial completion does not qualify for certification or refunds.
