Meet Your Instructor

Matthew Stanley

Army National Guard infantry veteran with 13 years of experience in military contracting, security operations, and intelligence analysis. Matthew has conducted operations across multiple continents — from NATO peacekeeping to maritime anti-piracy missions to combat/advisory roles in Ukraine.

His experience spans assault operations during the Kharkiv counteroffensive, waterborne assault operations in Enerhodar, and long-range operations in Zaporizhia Oblast. He has trained and fought alongside Ukrainian Armed Forces and GUR special operations elements in some of the most contested environments in modern warfare.

At Eastern Front Defense Academy, you receive instruction from someone who has tested these skills under fire.
